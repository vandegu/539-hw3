About Me

Who Am I?

I'm a data scientist, stats geek, analyst, husband, and lifelong gamer.

Where Am I?

A native of the Midwest, I have lived all over but currently reside in Ann Arbor, Michigan.

What Do I Do?

I love narrative, be it from a book, movie, or game. I also love data. Put the two together and my passion is to create stories from numbers in order to drive business and content decisions. I am also a creative person myself, and I enjoy learning about and developing tools and dashboards that people can use to wrest the meaning from complex data.

If you have a data that needs curious, creative, curated handling, look no further.

A little more about me...

My background is in academia. I worked as an atmospheric scientist before I realized that I wanted to use my computer and mathematical knowledge to work directly with customers, data, products, and development. Now, my dream is to combine my passion for content development with data science to drive business decisions in a creative industry.

In my free time, you will likely find me playing piano, composing music, watching movies (probably Star Wars) with my family, reading, or enjoying a board/video game with friends.
